scn 05 / method

How we direct the machine.

AI is not the director. It is the camera, the crew, the set, and the first draft. Direction is still the work.

01

Feeling first.

Before references or prompts, we define the emotional aftertaste. What should the viewer carry with them after the cut?

02

World bible.

Palette, lens, lighting logic, texture, wardrobe, surfaces, camera behaviour, and the rules the world cannot break.

03

Shot authorship.

Every frame is composed, rejected, reworked, and finished like a shot on set. Nothing is accepted because it merely looks generated.

04

Motion direction.

Pace, pause, velocity, weight, focus, transition, silence. Motion is how an image becomes memory.

05

Finish like film.

Color, typography, sound direction, grading notes, cleanup, and final delivery. Done is when the work stops asking for changes.

scn 06 / visual system

Premium is not polish. It is restraint.

We keep the interface cinematic: black space, copper accents, glass depth, slow parallax, hard cuts, lens metadata, and motion that behaves like a camera, not a website template.
